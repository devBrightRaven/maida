Kamae is the ready stance before drawing the sword. In tea ceremony, it is the quiet arrangement of space before the guest arrives. No kamae, no rin. You must prepare before you can face.

构え是拔刀前的准备架式。在茶道中， 是客人到来前对空间的静静安排。 没有构え就不能临む。你必须先准备，才能面对。

No recommendations. No tracking playtime. No streaks, achievements, or "you did great today." No notifications to bring you back. No social features. No data uploaded anywhere. All behavioral data stays on your machine.

The ideal user is someone who, one day, no longer needs Maida. If the tool becomes "very smart" or "very considerate," it has failed. Maida exists not to improve you, but to not stand between you and the world you want to enter.

沒有推薦。不追蹤遊玩時間。沒有連續登入、成就、或「你今天做得很好」。沒有通知叫你回來。沒有社交功能。資料不會上傳到任何地方。所有行為資料都留在你的裝置上。

理想的使用者是有一天不再需要 Maida 的人。如果這個工具變得「很聰明」或「很體貼」，那就是失敗。Maida 的存在不是為了改善你，而是為了不擋在你和你想進入的世界之間。
